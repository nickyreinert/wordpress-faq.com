Frequently Asked Questions

Why am I seeing a fatal error after updating GeneratePress?
Fatal errors often occur due to conflicts with other plugins or an incompatible PHP version running on your server.
Is SEOPress better than Yoast SEO?
SEOPress is often preferred for its lack of ads, lower price point, and extensive feature set included in the free version.
Is Bricks Builder a plugin or a theme?
Bricks Builder is a WordPress theme, not a plugin, which is a common point of confusion for new users.
Is WPML better than Polylang?
WPML offers more advanced features for complex enterprise sites, while Polylang is often preferred for simpler, lightweight multilingual setups.
Why are my emails not sending with WP Mail SMTP?
This is usually caused by incorrect SMTP configuration or authentication settings that do not match your email provider's requirements.
How do I check for Divi plugin conflicts?
Deactivate all plugins except Divi and re-enable them one by one to identify which specific plugin causes the conflict.
