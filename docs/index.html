Compatibility Guides

  • Elementor vs Oxygen Builder Compatibility & Comparison — Elementor and Oxygen Builder are separate page builders that cannot be used together on the same page, though they can coexist on a single site.
  • Elementor and SEOPress Compatibility Guide — Elementor and SEOPress are fully compatible, with SEOPress offering seamless integration to analyze content directly within the Elementor editor.
  • Elementor and Rank Math Compatibility Guide — Elementor and Rank Math are fully compatible, though some users recommend lighter SEO alternatives to prevent performance bloat.
  • Elementor and Ninja Forms Compatibility Guide — Elementor and Ninja Forms are fully compatible, serving as a reliable alternative when Elementor's native form widget lacks advanced features.
  • Elementor and Kadence Compatibility Guide — While technically possible to use both, combining Elementor and Kadence often leads to layout conflicts and performance bloat, so choosing one is highly recommended.
  • Elementor and Hello Elementor Theme Compatibility Guide — Hello Elementor is the official, lightweight blank canvas theme designed for seamless compatibility and optimal performance with the Elementor page builder.
  • Elementor and Gravity Forms Compatibility Guide — Elementor and Gravity Forms are highly compatible, with native widgets and third-party addons making integration and styling seamless.
  • Elementor and Fluent Forms Compatibility Guide — Elementor and Fluent Forms are highly compatible, though minor mobile layout or scroll offset issues may require custom CSS or JavaScript tweaks.
  • Elementor and Flatsome Compatibility Guide — While technically compatible, using Elementor with Flatsome is highly redundant and not recommended because Flatsome relies on its own proprietary UX Builder.
  • Elementor and All in One SEO Compatibility Guide — Elementor and All in One SEO (AIOSEO) are fully compatible, allowing users to optimize SEO settings directly within the Elementor editor interface.
  • Easy Digital Downloads and Elementor Compatibility Guide — Elementor is fully compatible with Easy Digital Downloads, especially when paired with lightweight themes like Hello Elementor or Astra.
  • Blocksy and Elementor Compatibility Guide — While Blocksy and Elementor are fully compatible, the community warns they can impact site speed, often preferring Gutenberg with Blocksy.
