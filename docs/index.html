Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use All-in-One WP Migration to move an Elementor site?
Yes, All-in-One WP Migration is commonly used to export and import WordPress sites built with Elementor.
Can I use Elementor and Oxygen Builder together on the same WordPress site?
While you can technically activate both, it is highly discouraged due to severe performance bloat and potential layout conflicts. They should not be used on the same page.
Are Elementor and WPML compatible?
Yes, they are compatible, but users often report that the combination creates significant performance issues and requires careful optimization.
Is WP Super Cache compatible with Elementor?
Yes, WP Super Cache is compatible with Elementor. However, you must ensure that the Elementor editor pages are excluded from caching to prevent editing conflicts.
Are Elementor and WP Mail SMTP compatible?
Yes, Elementor and WP Mail SMTP are fully compatible as they operate in different layers of the WordPress ecosystem.
Are Elementor and WooCommerce Subscriptions compatible?
Yes, they are compatible. Elementor allows you to design custom product pages and checkout flows that work seamlessly with WooCommerce Subscriptions.

Compatibility Guides

  • Elementor and All-in-One WP Migration Compatibility Elementor and All-in-One WP Migration are generally compatible, provided that site security and file integrity are maintained during the transfer.
  • Elementor vs Oxygen Builder Compatibility & Comparison Elementor and Oxygen Builder are separate page builders that cannot be used together on the same page, though they can coexist on a single site.
  • Elementor and WPML Compatibility Guide While Elementor and WPML are technically compatible, the combination is frequently cited as a major source of site bloat and performance degradation.
  • Elementor and WP Super Cache Compatibility Guide Elementor and WP Super Cache are fully compatible, though users should exclude Elementor's query strings and avoid caching the active page builder interface.
  • Elementor and WP Mail SMTP Compatibility Elementor and WP Mail SMTP are fully compatible and function together without any reported technical conflicts in standard WordPress environments.
  • Elementor and WooCommerce Subscriptions Compatibility Elementor and WooCommerce Subscriptions are fully compatible when using standard WooCommerce-ready themes and proper plugin configurations.
  • Elementor and W3 Total Cache Compatibility Guide Elementor and W3 Total Cache are fully compatible, but require careful configuration of minification and page caching to avoid breaking the page builder interface.
  • Elementor and UpdraftPlus Compatibility Guide Elementor and UpdraftPlus are highly compatible, with no known direct conflicts, making UpdraftPlus an excellent choice for backing up Elementor sites.
