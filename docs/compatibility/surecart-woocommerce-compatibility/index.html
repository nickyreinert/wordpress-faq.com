Community Q&A

Can I use SureCart and WooCommerce together?

It is generally advised against as both plugins attempt to control shop pages and checkout flows, leading to significant site conflicts.

How do I fix SureCart shop page conflicts?

The most effective solution is to deactivate WooCommerce if you intend to use SureCart as your primary e-commerce platform.
