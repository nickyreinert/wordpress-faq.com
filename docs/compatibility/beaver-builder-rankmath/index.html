Community Q&A

Does Rank Math work with Beaver Builder?

Yes, Rank Math is fully compatible with Beaver Builder and allows you to optimize your page content directly within the builder interface.

How do I add Rank Math SEO to Beaver Builder pages?

You can access the Rank Math SEO settings via the WordPress admin bar while editing your page in Beaver Builder or through the post editor.
