Known Issues

  • Combine Files option in WP Rocket may cause rendering issues with Elementor widgets

Community Q&A

Are Elementor and WP Rocket compatible?

Yes, Elementor and WP Rocket are fully compatible. WP Rocket helps optimize the performance of Elementor-built sites by caching pages and minifying files.

How do I prevent WP Rocket from breaking my Elementor pages?

To avoid layout conflicts, exclude Elementor’s CSS and JS files from minification or deferral settings in WP Rocket if you notice broken designs.
