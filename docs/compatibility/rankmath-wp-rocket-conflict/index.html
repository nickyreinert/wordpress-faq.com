Community Q&A

Why does Rank Math sitemap keep getting deleted?

It is often caused by caching plugins like WP Rocket that cache the sitemap file, leading to conflicts and deletion.

How do I fix Rank Math sitemap conflict with WP Rocket?

You must add your sitemap URL to the ‘Never Cache URLs’ section in the WP Rocket settings to prevent the conflict.
