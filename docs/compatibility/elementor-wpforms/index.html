Community Q&A

Are Elementor and WPForms compatible?

Yes, WPForms is fully compatible with Elementor and includes a dedicated widget to easily embed forms into your Elementor layouts.

How do I customize WPForms email templates in Elementor?

WPForms features a native drag-and-drop email template builder, allowing you to customize notifications without needing external Elementor add-ons.
