Community Q&A

Is WPML better than Polylang?

WPML offers more advanced features for complex enterprise sites, while Polylang is often preferred for simpler, lightweight multilingual setups.

Does WPML slow down WordPress?

WPML can impact performance on very large sites due to its extensive database queries, but it is generally well-optimized for standard usage.
