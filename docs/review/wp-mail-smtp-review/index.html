Community Q&A

Why are my emails not sending with WP Mail SMTP?

This is usually caused by incorrect SMTP configuration or authentication settings that do not match your email provider’s requirements.

How do I fix WP Mail SMTP configuration errors?

Ensure your SMTP host, port, and authentication credentials are correct and that your hosting provider does not block outgoing SMTP connections.
