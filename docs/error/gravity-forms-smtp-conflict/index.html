Community Q&A

Why are my Gravity Forms notifications not sending?

WordPress default mail functions are often blocked by hosts; using an SMTP plugin ensures emails are sent via authenticated services like Office 365.

How do I fix Gravity Forms email issues with Office 365?

Install a reliable SMTP plugin, enter your Office 365 credentials, and configure the plugin to route all outgoing WordPress mail through your account.
