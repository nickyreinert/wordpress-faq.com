Community Q&A

How do I make my Blocksy site load faster?

Enable the built-in performance settings in the customizer, optimize your images, and limit the number of third-party plugins installed.

Does Blocksy work well with Elementor?

Yes, Blocksy is compatible with Elementor, but using too many heavy widgets can impact your site’s overall performance and load speed.
