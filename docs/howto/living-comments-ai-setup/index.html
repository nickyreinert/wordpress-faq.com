Community Q&A

How do I enable comments in WordPress?

Navigate to Settings > Discussion in your WordPress dashboard and check the box labeled Allow people to submit comments on new posts.

Are AI comment plugins recommended for WordPress?

Most community members advise against AI-generated comments as they can be flagged as spam and negatively impact user trust.
