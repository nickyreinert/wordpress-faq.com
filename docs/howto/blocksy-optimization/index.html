Community Q&A

How do I make my Blocksy site load faster?

Enable the built-in performance modules in the Blocksy customizer, optimize your images, and minimize the use of heavy third-party page builders.

Does Blocksy work well with Elementor?

Yes, Blocksy is compatible with Elementor, but using too many complex widgets can impact your site’s overall speed and performance.
