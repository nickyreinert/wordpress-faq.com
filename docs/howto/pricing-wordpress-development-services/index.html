Community Q&A

How do I calculate hourly rates for WordPress development?

Calculate your desired annual income, overhead costs, and billable hours to determine a sustainable hourly rate that covers your business expenses.

Should I charge hourly or per project?

Hourly is safer for undefined scopes, while project-based pricing rewards efficiency and is often preferred by clients for budget predictability.

What factors influence WordPress development pricing?

Complexity, timeline, custom functionality requirements, and the level of ongoing maintenance or support needed are primary cost drivers.
