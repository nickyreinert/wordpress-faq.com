Community Q&A

How can I improve WooCommerce Subscriptions performance?

You can improve performance by offloading Action Scheduler tasks to a dedicated queue runner and regularly cleaning up completed subscription logs.

Does caching affect WooCommerce Subscriptions?

Yes, aggressive page caching can break dynamic subscription content; ensure your checkout and account pages are excluded from full-page caching.
