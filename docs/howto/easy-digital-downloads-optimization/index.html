Community Q&A

How can I speed up Easy Digital Downloads?

Implement object caching, optimize your database tables, and ensure your hosting environment supports high-concurrency PHP processes.

Does caching break Easy Digital Downloads?

Yes, if not configured correctly. You must exclude the checkout, cart, and account pages from page caching to prevent session issues.
